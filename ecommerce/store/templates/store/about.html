A little bit about us...

We couldn't be prouder to open the doors of our very first store, located at 10 Prince Street just a few blocks from the SoHo office where our team dreams up all of our pieces. Designed in collaboration with our friend, Poonam, this space is full of our favorite design elements, from a beautiful custom seating area perfect for trying on our footwear to the pleated tissue paper used during the pleating process in our own signature heels. If you're in the area, we hope you stop by for a visit and find something you love.
